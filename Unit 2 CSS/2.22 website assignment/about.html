About the Company

... and its Founders

Byte the Bullet™ is a computer store offering the best and fastest computers around! We have computers for all types of buyers including students, office workers, and gamers. All of our products have affordable* prices and are made of organic materials** - no living things were harmed in the process of making these products***.

*The MacBook Pro isn't necessarily affordable but other than Apple products everything else is reasonably priced
**Technically anything is organic if you think hard enough
***Maybe a few...

Logo & Name Explanation: The logo is a mouse in a shopping cart. This is used to symbolize that we are a computer store. It's kind of ironic in the sense that the logo has a mouse but the three products we're currenty selling don't come with any peripherals. The name Byte the Bullet™ comes from the digital information unit "byte" and the phrase "bite the bullet" which means to do something difficult.
